Frequently Asked Questions

At eLearning College, we take pride in welcoming ambitious learners from all backgrounds. Our free online courses can be taken from any location, with no deadlines, no time restrictions, and no fixed study schedules. Upon completion of your course, you will have the option of claiming an endorsed certificate or diploma for an additional fee. However, there is no obligation to do so all of our courses are 100% free of charge.

You can take only two (2x) of our free online courses as you like, but we ask that you complete your current course before enrolling on your next course. If you change your mind about any course you sign up for before its completion, contact the eLearning College support team to discuss switching to a new programme.

Upon completion of your free online course, you will have the opportunity to request one of the following certificates as proof of your new skills: 1. An official Course Completion Certificate from eLearning College 2. A CPD Accredited Certificate to boost your CPD profile 3. An Endorsed Certificate issued by the Quality Licence Scheme
